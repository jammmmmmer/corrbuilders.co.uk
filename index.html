About Us

We Build With Pride

CorrBuilders was founded on a single belief: every client deserves a build they're genuinely proud of. Over 15 years, we've grown from a small crew into one of the most trusted residential builders in Islington, Stoke Newington and across North London — without losing the personal service that got us here.

We employ our tradespeople directly — no subcontractor chains, no strangers on your site. Every project is personally overseen by our senior team from first spade to final snag. No corners cut. No surprises on the invoice.

Fully Insured & Accredited

Public liability and employer's liability insurance on every project, as standard.

Fixed Quotes — No Hidden Costs

What we quote is what you pay. Transparent pricing from day one, in writing.

10-Year Workmanship Guarantee

We stand behind our work long after the last brick is laid — in writing.
